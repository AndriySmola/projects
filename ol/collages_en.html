"Collages"

Description of the series:

Collage is a unique artistic technique that involves creating a composition by combining various materials: pieces of newspapers, posters, wallpaper, photographs, and other objects with different textures and colors. It is not only a creative process but also a relatively easy way of artistic expression.

A collage allows you to tell about yourself in the language of images, to convey emotions, feelings, and mood. Each of my works is a part of my personal emotional space, an internal dialog with myself and the world.
