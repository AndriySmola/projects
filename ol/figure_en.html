"Shapes"

Description of the series:

The “ Shapes ” series presents photographs of simple objects such as a plastic fork, spoon and cup, shot from unusual angles.

Each photograph emphasizes the uniqueness of shadows created by the interaction of light and simple shapes. Unusual shooting angles allow us to see familiar objects in a new light, revealing their ability to create unexpected and creative compositions.

The photographs emphasize the contrast between light and shadow, which adds depth and drama to the images. The simplicity of the objects emphasizes the complexity and versatility of the shadows they create, turning everyday things into artistic objects.

The “ Shapes ” series explores aesthetics in the everyday, demonstrating how simple objects can become a source of inspiration and visual pleasure through the play of light and shadow.
