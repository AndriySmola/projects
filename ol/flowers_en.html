"Floral layering"

Description of the series:

The photographic series “Floral layering” is my exploration of the natural world through the lens of my camera. In the process, I use various creative approaches to create breathtaking floral compositions that reveal the multi-layered beauty and perfection of nature.

These fantastic images of flowers on the verge of reality and fiction are striking in their depth and colorfulness. Each frame of this series is my journey into the world of flora, where nature becomes the basis for my art, and “Flower layering” allows me to reveal the unique beauty of this world to the viewers.
