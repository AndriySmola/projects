"Household objects in a color field"

Description of the series:

The series of photographs “Household objects in the color field” explores the harmony between familiar objects and their surroundings through the play of color and light.

In the photographs, everyday objects, such as sponges, are transformed into abstract forms, losing their utilitarian function and becoming elements of artistic composition.

Thanks to the bright background and sustained color palettes, these objects acquire new meanings and look like a part of an experimental color field.

The series makes the viewer think about the hidden beauty of everyday life, giving ordinary things a new context and expressiveness, turning them into works of art.
