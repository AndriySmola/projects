Artist Statement

As long as I can remember, I have dreamed of painting. As a little girl, I admired my mother's embroidery - her towels resembled blooming flower beds. When I was 57, I told myself I could do it. I can also convey the colors of life. I snatch pictures from everyday life and show their beauty.

Artist Statement

My models are objects around us that we are used to not noticing. With my work, I not only invite you to my own explorations of the world and yourself, but also convey moments of delight that help you see simple things in a new way.

Artist Statement

What inspires me when creating my works? Interesting form, color, texture and their interaction, as well as a non-banal combination of simple objects in the space of the color field.

Life story

I, Olena Levchenko, was born and have lived in Kyiv, Ukraine, all my life. My life credo is learning. I have always loved and love creative approaches in everything, regardless of whether it is a profession or a hobby. The art of photography has fascinated me since 2019 and gives me the opportunity to capture interesting moments of our lives and express my creative personality. I shoot a lot and have interesting photo projects. I am looking for myself in different styles of photography. I like to work with color, explore different forms and their interaction. Participant in many photography exhibitions, biennials and online exhibitions. Winner of the PH3 2024 competition and the BIFA 2024 competition.
