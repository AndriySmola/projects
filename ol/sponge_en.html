Mondrian Sponges : "Geometry of everyday life"

Project description and gallery:

The creation of this series allowed me to escape from the war news and missile attacks, to regain my psychological balance and come back to a more or less normal life. Every day, creating a composition for the next photo, I treated myself with color, studied the harmonies of combination and interaction of textures and shapes.

Geometry of everyday life

The Mondrian Sponges series represents for me an original interpretation of the famous neo-plasticism style created by Piet Mondrian.

Geometry of everyday life

Instead of traditional paints and canvases, I used kitchen sponges, which are arranged in such a way that they resemble the artist's iconic works.

Geometry of everyday life

The bright colors and textures of the sponges create the impression of playing with perception, connecting the world of art and everyday objects.

Geometry of everyday life

This series of photographs emphasizes aesthetics in ordinary things and encourages us to see beauty even in such simple objects as dishwashing sponges.
