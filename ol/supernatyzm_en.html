"In search of Suprematism"

Description of the series:

The series “In Search of Suprematism” reflects my fascination with Suprematism and Malevich's works.

When I was shooting a series of works, I saw signs of Suprematism in the simple, post-Soviet modernist architectural structures of the playground: simple geometric shapes, lines, circles, rectangles, bright textured multi-layered paint color surrounded by white space.
